# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Albertina, a junior developer creating her professional portfolio. Site visitors are potential employers, recruiters, and fellow developers evaluating her skills, project quality, and design sensibility.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio that presents Albertina's identity, technical projects, and professional trajectory as a junior developer. Success means visitors leave with a clear sense of who she is, what she builds, and how she thinks.

## Brand Personality

Elegant · Warm · Intentional

Voice is confident but approachable — a junior developer who takes craft seriously without pretense. The portfolio should feel like a well-made personal site, not a corporate landing page or a template.

## Anti-references

Avoid looking like a generic SaaS startup page, a dark-mode terminal aesthetic, or an over-designed flashy site. No stock photography, no hero-metric templates, no glassmorphism. The site should look "normal for a student" — but refined, with deliberate choices that signal care and taste. No evidence of template origins.

## Design Principles

- **Craft over flash** — Every visual choice must earn its place. No decoration for its own sake.
- **Warm professionalism** — Approachable and human, not cold or corporate, but still polished and intentional.
- **Show the work** — Projects and skills are the centerpiece. Design serves the content, not the other way around.
- **Honest technology** — The tech stack (HTML + CSS + PHP + JS) is straightforward and transparent. No unnecessary frameworks.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA compliance. Adequate color contrast, keyboard navigable, semantic HTML, reduced motion respected.
